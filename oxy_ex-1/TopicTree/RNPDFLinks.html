Maintenace/Hot Fix Release Notes

Welcome to the Maintenance Release Notes section of Blume Global's Online Help Site. This dedicated space serves as a comprehensive resource for users seeking detailed information on the latest updates, enhancements, and fixes implemented across our suite of logistics and supply chain management solutions.

Our Maintenance Release Notes are meticulously prepared and published in PDF format to ensure that you have access to well-organized, searchable, and printable documents. Whether you are a system administrator, an end-user, or a stakeholder within the logistics and supply chain ecosystem, these documents are designed to provide you with a clear understanding of the changes introduced in each release, the benefits of these changes, and how they may impact your daily operations.

What You Can Expect to Find

  • Detailed Descriptions: Each set of release notes includes in-depth explanations of new features, improvements, and any issues that have been resolved. This ensures you are well-informed about the capabilities of your solutions.

  • Version Information: Clear identification of the software version to help you track the progression of the product and ensure compatibility with your systems.

  • Implementation Guidance: Where applicable, we provide guidance on how to implement new features or changes to ensure a smooth transition and to maximize the benefits of the updates.

  • Known Issues and Limitations: Transparency is key to us. We include any known challenges or limitations that are being addressed, offering workarounds where possible to help you maintain operational efficiency.

  • Supporting Resources: Links to additional resources, such as user manuals, technical documentation, and support contact information, to assist you in leveraging the full potential of our solutions.

Our aim is to ensure that you have all the necessary information to optimize your use of Blume Global products and services. We encourage you to review these notes regularly to stay informed about the latest enhancements and to help plan your maintenance schedules effectively.

For ease of access, the release notes are categorized by product and release date. We recommend bookmarking this page for quick reference and subscribing to our notification service to receive updates when new Maintenance Release Notes are available.
